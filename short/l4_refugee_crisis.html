L.4 Refugee crisis
Thousands of people each year are displaced because of man-made and natural disasters. Anoushka is one such, having been forced to flee her village along with her mother and younger brother. They reach an IFRC center just across the border in a relatively safe area, but with no documentation. Since the government of her homeland is in turmoil, there is no way for the IFRC staff to easily establish their identities. Fortunately, Anoushka had been issued a self-sovereign proof of birth, attached to which is the proof of birth and marriage for her parents. She is able to retrieve this because it is available from many places over the Internet. Since it is verifiable, the IFRC is comfortable vouching for them and resettling them in a safer area for the duration of the conflict.
